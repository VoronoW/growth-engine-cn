# Cross-Border Growth Diagnosis Framework

A diagnostic framework used by cross-border growth consultants to quickly identify the core growth bottlenecks inside an e-commerce or global brand expansion company.

The questions below are designed for founder interviews, internal audits, and growth consulting projects.

---

## Traffic Structure

**Diagnostic Questions**

1. What percentage of your total traffic comes from paid advertising versus organic channels?
2. If all advertising stopped today, how much would your daily orders decline within 7 days?
3. Does any single platform (Amazon, TikTok, Meta, etc.) contribute more than 70% of your total traffic?
4. Are you able to track traffic sources clearly across different channels (ads, organic, influencers, referrals)?
5. Is your organic traffic (SEO, content, social media) growing, stable, or declining over the past 6 months?

---

## Advertising Efficiency

**Diagnostic Questions**

1. Is your reported ROAS aligned with your real profit margin after all costs?
2. Can you distinguish between **new customer conversions** and **returning customer purchases** in your ad reporting?
3. What percentage of your advertising budget is allocated to testing new creatives or audiences?
4. Are there ad campaigns with high spending but consistently low conversion rates that are still running?
5. Do you follow a structured testing framework for ads (creative testing, audience testing, product testing)?

---

## SKU Strategy

**Diagnostic Questions**

1. How many active SKUs does your company currently sell?
2. What percentage of total revenue is generated by your top 20% SKUs?
3. Do you clearly classify products into categories such as **traffic drivers, profit products, and long-tail items**?
4. Do you track advertising ROI at the SKU level?
5. Do you have a clear lifecycle management process for products (launch, growth, maturity, phase-out)?

---

## Content & Creative System

**Diagnostic Questions**

1. How many new ad creatives or content pieces are produced each month?
2. Do your creatives follow a structured script framework (problem, scenario, solution, proof)?
3. Are creative performance metrics such as CTR and CVR tracked and reviewed regularly?
4. Do you maintain a centralized creative library or asset database?
5. Is there a standardized process or team responsible for continuous creative production?

---

## Customer Asset Ownership

**Diagnostic Questions**

1. Do you own direct customer contact data (email, phone, WhatsApp, community groups)?
2. What percentage of your orders come from repeat customers?
3. Do you maintain a customer segmentation or tagging system?
4. Do you run lifecycle marketing campaigns (new customer, active customer, churn recovery)?
5. Do you actively operate retention channels such as email marketing, loyalty programs, or communities?

---

## Data Attribution & Infrastructure

**Diagnostic Questions**

1. Do you use tracking systems such as GTM, UTM parameters, or server-side tracking?
2. Is there a significant discrepancy between ad platform tracking data and actual sales data?
3. Can you monitor sales performance at the SKU level across channels?
4. Can you track the full funnel from **ad click → product page → purchase → repeat purchase**?
5. Do you have a centralized BI dashboard that aggregates key business metrics?

---

## Channel Dependency

**Diagnostic Questions**

1. Does a single platform account for more than 70% of your company’s revenue?
2. Are you currently selling through multiple channels (Amazon, TikTok Shop, Shopify, etc.)?
3. Do you own at least one independent traffic source such as a website or social media community?
4. Would a platform policy change significantly impact your business performance?
5. Do you conduct periodic risk assessments for platform dependency?

---

## Team Growth Capability

**Diagnostic Questions**

1. Does your advertising performance rely heavily on a single key employee?
2. Are there documented SOPs for advertising operations and growth processes?
3. Does the team conduct regular data review or growth strategy meetings?
4. Is there cross-department collaboration between product, marketing, and operations teams?
5. Can the team independently design and run growth experiments?

---

## Growth Experimentation System

**Diagnostic Questions**

1. How many growth experiments or tests does your team run each month?
2. Do you follow a structured A/B testing framework?
3. Are test hypotheses and results documented and reviewed?
4. Is there a fixed budget allocation for experimentation?
5. Are successful growth experiments turned into repeatable playbooks?

---

## Brand Asset & Long-Term Growth

**Diagnostic Questions**

1. Is your brand search volume increasing over time?
2. Do you operate active brand-owned social media accounts?
3. Do you have a clearly defined brand positioning and visual identity system?
4. Do you collaborate with influencers or creators to build brand awareness?
5. Do customers purchase your products primarily because of brand recognition rather than price?
