# STYLE-NOTES.md

## Brand Summary
`峥锐 ZAPEX` should feel like a cross-border growth operating system delivered by a premium consulting team.

The site should communicate:
- strategic judgment
- execution discipline
- data-backed growth credibility
- calm control under complexity

## Most Recognizable Signals
- Consulting-grade Chinese copy with system-oriented framing
- Modular sections that read like operating layers, not ad sections
- Restrained palette with one strong accent used intentionally
- Clean, modern interface with subtle SaaS structure
- Calm but serious conversion points
- Intake and work-surface patterns that imply real execution, not just storytelling

## Reference Interpretation
- Borrow precise hierarchy in framework modules.
- Borrow low-friction guidance in intake and scheduling moments.
- Borrow modular composability in content and knowledge-heavy surfaces.
- Borrow restraint and finish in premium presentation moments.

## What This Brand Is Not
- Not a marketing agency portfolio
- Not a consumer-tech launch site
- Not an AI neon product
- Not a heavy enterprise admin console

## Distinguishing Signals
- Strategic module names should feel like systems, engines, layers, or paths.
- Sections should imply diagnostic progression and execution logic.
- Interface density should shift by purpose: tighter for frameworks, lighter for trust and CTA.
- UI should feel modern and premium, but more operational than luxurious.
- Content should feel designed for decision-makers, not trend-chasing founders.
- CTA language should sound like controlled next steps, not sales pressure.
